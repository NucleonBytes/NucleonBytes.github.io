What is NitroJS and NitrixCore?

NitroJS is a Javascript-driven runtime, which gives NitrixCore direct access to the system, enabling it to imitate native speeds while retaining the awesomeness and capabilities of web technologies. The NitrixCore is what prepares and renders the contents. NitrixCore is based off of Chromium and it's V8 Javascript engine
