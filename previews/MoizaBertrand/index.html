MOIZA BERTRAND


Recent Work!

Personal Statement: Theatre has been a mainstay and a delight, and I can think of nothing better than pursuing a career in this industry. In Drama I have learnt a variety of skills such as, team work, improvisation and commitment, as well as movement, voice, gesture and facial expression to help portray my character when performing. Drama and Theatre studies have taught me how to work with others; students, younger and older than me, as well as teachers. After doing four school productions, playing the lead role in two of them, I fell in love with the atmosphere and buzz theatre gave me. I was able to develop my skills of improvisation through monologue and given circumstances. By devising a performance as well as helping younger students study and devise a piece of their own. Taking Music BTEC, has helped me understand the music industry in depth. Across the 2 years I have learnt and developed a contrast of skills, including composition, listening and devising. I have learnt to analyse these skills in depth and put them into action. I had never composed a piece of music before tackling this course, but now, I can create my own piece. Being able to accept mistakes and learning from them has made me a stronger performer. As well as these subjects I took on Film Studies. This has helped me enhance my time management, planning and research skill; I had to learn to plan to give myself time to shoot and edit in time to meet the deadlines. Drama and Film Studies have allowed me to evaluate the characters as well as performances in details. My knowledge of film has grown rapidly due to researching the industry. Participation in open evenings enables me to communicate with people of all ages and backgrounds and be an ambassador for my school. Extracurricular activities such as Gymnastics and Dance have further developed my performance skills. I perform traditional dance at events such as weddings, engagements and parties with my very own Sega Dance Company. I am looking forward to showing my commitment and pursuing a career in this industry. I love meeting new people and this will be a great opportunity for me to use my communication skills and make new friends.
