Five easy steps to success


1: Get in touch

Get in touch with us today by going to the Contact Us page or by calling 00000000000 if you'd like to chat first. Alternatively, you can email us at email@FaithValleyTuition.com


2: Choose the subject and time

Choose the subject and hour (length) that suits your needs.


3: Attend a taster session

Once you have chosen your subject, come along for a taster session. All you need is pen and paper.


4: Fill in the sign up form

Once you are at the taster session, you will be given a sheet to fill in. Once it is filled in, you are officially a learner at Faith Valley Groups Tuition Centre.


5: Attend weekly and see results

Congratulations! You are a learner at Faith Valley Groups Tuition Centre. Now all you need to do is attend. You'll be amazed at how rapidly you'll gain confindence, independence, and better results at school.
