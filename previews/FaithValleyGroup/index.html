Students are Welcome at Faith Valley Group Tuition Center


Faith Valley Group Tuition offers the highest quality tuition services to students of Primary, Secondary, and Further Education levels. To ensure our students exceed expectations and maintain a high level of achievements, we constantly review our courses and methods of delivery, matching requirements of school syllabus and examinations. We constantly record and analyse student progress to ensure that we provide appropriate resources and tailor-made notes to supplement what the students are learning in school.


We believe that each student has unique potential. Faith Valley Group Tuition strives to bring out the best in your child academically. We believe that by employing the appropriate teaching methods, carefully selected for each individual, we can help your child to enjoy learning and develop his or her potential. We keep in very close contacts with parents, ensuring they are kept to date with their child's progress.
