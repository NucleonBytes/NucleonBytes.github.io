Background of Supplementary School


Faith Valley Tuition was established by a group of teachers and examiners with the intention of helping counter underachievement, improve attainment and challenge the high ability to extend their learning specifically in English, Maths, Science and German from KS1, to KS5.


The school adopts teaching and learning strategies that help to establish a well grounded platform to engage and empower learners, with supplementary support in the subjects listed so they can excel academically.


Faith Valley Tuition believes that every learner, given the right environment and opportunities, is capable of achieving success. With the right knowledge and expertise, the trained teacher can always make an invaluable impact in the life of a learner.Through teaching, Faith Valley Tuition believes it is able to share knowledge with those in pursuit knowledge and help them excel.


Our objective in teaching is to assist learners to pursue and acquire knowledge, improve their attainment levels, and achieve and maximize their potential to the fullest.

Our Mission Statement


To make a positive contribution to the lives of young people by increasing their life chances through education that enables them to achieve their full potential


Faith Valley Tuition believes that every child and young person must have an education that enables them to achieve their full potential. We define ‘education’ not just in terms of a school’s core curriculum subjects – however important these are – but also in terms of developing healthy, resilient and well-rounded young people, ready for responsible adult life.


Faith Valley Tuition aims to achieve this by enabling young people to recognise and fulfill their potential through developing skills, confidence and aspirations. Faith Valley Tuition aims to raise the standard of education in the community by creating a progressive caring and high quality learning environment for all its learners.
